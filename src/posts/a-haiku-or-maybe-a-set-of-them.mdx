I wrote a haiku (or a set of them meant to be read as one) after a good shower. I hate ticks.

<br />
>longing is dying
>
>these two are one in the same
>
>a dangerous game
>
>—
>
>loving is worth it
>
>to long is only to miss
>
>proving love was real
>
>—
>
>give yourself some time
>
>put the love to rest and be
>
>let memories breathe
>
>—
>
>go seldom back there
>
>go easy on the past, for
>
>longing is dying 
>
<br />

I hope that you enjoy reading it as much as I did writing it.
