despite the cynicism i find within myself lately, i find that it’s good to take a step back and remind myself that each new day is a gift. it is imperative that we do this to remind ourselves that though life may look bleak in some moments, there are great parts to this wild experience that is the roller coaster of life. it has fucking hands. that being said, it’s the people. it’s the love. it’s why we do it.

i am incredibly lucky to have so many friends and people i love in my life. things suck, and we all know it, but without these people, i *personally* would go crazy.

each new day is a gift, and this really serves as a love letter to the people who keep me sane and laughing on these days.

laugh or cry, man.
